Privacy Policy  |  Effective: 2026-06-16

This app detects nearby Bluetooth trackers (such as AirTag, SmartTag, Tile, and Google) and warns you about unfamiliar tags that may be following you.

1. What we collect

  • Bluetooth scan results (nearby tracker signals) — processed on your device to detect trackers that follow you.
  • Location — used on your device to judge whether a tag follows you across places. It is not uploaded for detection.
  • Family Safety (optional, off by default) — if you turn it on and connect family, an anonymous ID, a public key, and a notification token are stored to relay alerts. Any SOS location you choose to share is end-to-end encrypted; our servers cannot read it.

2. How we use it

  • Detection and risk scoring run on your phone.

3. No ads, no selling, no sharing

  • There are no ads in this app, and we do not use any advertising, analytics, or tracking tools (no third-party tracking SDKs).
  • We never sell your data, and we never hand it to other companies.
  • Almost everything stays on your phone. Data leaves your phone only in these cases: a Family SOS that you start yourself (end-to-end encrypted, so the relay server cannot read your alert or location); the minimum needed to run Family Safety when you enable it (an anonymous ID, a public key, a notification token); and map requests.
  • Maps: when you open a map or share an SOS location, map tiles, optional address lookup, and opening an external map app send your IP address and the map's location area to the map providers we use (OpenFreeMap, Esri, Mapbox, and Google Maps). Your scan and tracking history is never sent to them.

4. How the app uses your phone

  • Bluetooth — to scan for nearby trackers. The scan is filtered so the app only looks at tracker-type signals.
  • Location (only if you allow it) — read through your phone's system location, and used on the phone to judge whether a tag keeps following you across places.

5. Keeping or deleting your data

  • Your scan and location history stay only on your phone, for as long as the app is installed.
  • Background monitoring and Family Safety are optional and can be turned off anytime.
  • To delete everything, just uninstall the app — that removes the local data and disconnects any family links.

6. Your rights

You are in control of your data. You can ask us to:

  • show you what data relates to you,
  • delete your data, or
  • stop using it.

Since almost everything stays on your phone, uninstalling the app is the quickest way to remove it. For anything stored to run Family Safety, just email us and we'll help. These rights are recognized by laws such as the EU GDPR and Korea's Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA).

7. Children

  • This app is not made for children and does not knowingly collect data from children.

8. About the tracker brand names

We mention names like AirTag and Find My (Apple), Galaxy SmartTag (Samsung), Tile (Life360), and Find My Device (Google) only to tell you which kind of tracker was found. These names are trademarks of their owners. This is an independent app — it is not made, sponsored, or endorsed by any of these companies.

9. Who runs this app & contact

This app is made by an independent developer. For any privacy question or request (including the rights above), contact SOYU9815@GMAIL.COM.
